# Conversation Cards — Product Shot

## Subject Prompt

A deck of richly colored conversational prompt cards scattered playfully across a sunlit tabletop. The cards are thick matte cardstock — some in bold terracotta, others in deep forest green, sage, and warm aged gold — with cream-colored hand-lettered text. A few cards overlap, showing pops of color contrast. They sit on a wooden table next to a bright bowl of clementines, a small vase of fresh wildflowers in yellows and burnt orange, and a half-full glass of white wine catching the light. Warm golden afternoon sun pouring in, creating rich color saturation and playful light streaks across the scene. The composition feels lively and mid-gathering, as if friends just paused a conversation to refill their drinks.
